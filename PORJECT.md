# Project: MulanPaddlers Website

## Goal

Recruit new members, present team identity, and provide a professional, credible online presence for a women’s dragon boat team in the GTA.

## Success Criteria

* Clear value proposition within 5 seconds
* Obvious “Join the Team” CTA
* Mobile-first usability
* Bilingual (EN / ZH) parity
* Fast load, no broken assets, no console errors

## Audience

* Women interested in dragon boat (beginners → experienced)
* GTA community
* Potential sponsors/partners

## Design Reference
Primary visual reference: HTML5 UP "Dimension"
Reference: https://html5up.net/dimension

## Desired Visual Direction
- Single-page experience
- Full-screen landing layout
- Panel / modal-style content sections
- Minimal navigation
- Strong sense of depth
- Clean, immersive, modern presentation
- Responsive on desktop and mobile

## Important Note
This project should be inspired by the visual behavior and layout style of Dimension, but must NOT copy the original template code or rely on jQuery.

## Core Sections

* Home (hero + quick nav)
* About
* Training / Programs
* Gallery
* Sponsorship
* FAQ
* Contact

### Sponsorship Section Purpose

* Present sponsorship opportunities clearly
* Show team credibility (e.g., achievements, community impact)
* Provide simple sponsorship tiers overview
* Include clear contact CTA for sponsors

### Sponsorship Content Direction

* Professional tone (different from recruitment tone)
* Highlight:
    * Team mission and community impact
    * Competition participation
    * Visibility for sponsors (events, social media, gear)

### Home
* First impression, fast clarity, strong CTA

### About
* Who we are, why we exist, team values

### Training / Programs
* What we offer, structure, who it's for

### Gallery
* Visual proof of team activity and energy

### Sponsorship
* Professional pitch for partners and sponsors

### FAQ
* Remove friction and answer common questions

### Contact
* Simple and direct way to reach us + Social Media

## Interaction Model

* Single-page experience
* Dimension-inspired panel sections (overlay/slide panels)
* Smooth, restrained transitions
* Keyboard accessible (Esc to close panels)

## Constraints

* No jQuery
* No legacy template code
* Static-compatible (GitHub Pages)
* Maintainable structure (small components, clear data flow)

## Non-Goals

* No CMS in v1
* No server-side features (SSR/APIs)
* No heavy animation libraries unless justified
