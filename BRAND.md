# MulanPaddlers Brand

## Primary Colors

* Navy: #1E2A44
* Hot Pink: #FF4F9A

## Secondary Colors

* Soft Pink: #FFD1E6
* Light Gray: #F5F7FA
* White: #FFFFFF

## Usage

* Backgrounds: deep navy / dark gradients
* Primary actions (buttons/links): hot pink
* Text on dark: white or light gray
* Avoid muddy purples; keep contrast crisp

## Typography

* Clean sans-serif (system or Inter-like)
* Clear hierarchy: large hero, distinct section titles, readable body

## Logo

* Location: /public/logos
* Prefer SVG
* Maintain clear space; do not stretch
* Do not recolor unless approved

## Imagery

* Real team photos preferred
* Themes: water, motion, teamwork, strength
* Avoid generic corporate stock

## Visual Direction

* Athletic, modern, confident
* Minimal but intentional
* Emphasis on clarity over decoration

## Design Reference Style
The site should visually lean toward:
- immersive one-page presentation
- dark atmospheric background
- centered hero composition
- overlay / modal-like content panels
- minimal but confident UI
- subtle motion and depth, not flashy animation

## Visual Interpretation
Do not reproduce the reference exactly.
Use it as inspiration, then adapt it to fit MulanPaddlers branding:
