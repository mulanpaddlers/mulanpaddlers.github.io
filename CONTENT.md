## About

### English

Title: About MulanPaddlers

MulanPaddlers is an all-women dragon boat team based in the GTA.
We are committed to creating an inclusive and supportive environment where women of all backgrounds can discover the sport, build confidence, and form meaningful connections.

Our team brings together individuals with different levels of experience, united by a shared goal—to improve, compete, and grow together.

We value discipline, teamwork, and positive energy, while respecting different learning paces and personal goals.

---

## Programs / Training

### English

Title: Training & Programs

Our training focuses on technique, strength, endurance, and team coordination in a structured and supportive environment.

**Indoor Training (Winter / Spring)**

* Pool-based sessions and conditioning
* Technique fundamentals and refinement
* Strength and mobility development

**On-Water Season**

* Technical paddling training
* Boat timing and synchronization
* Race preparation and performance

**Trial & New Paddlers**

* Beginner-friendly introduction
* Learn basic techniques
* Experience the team before committing

We train with purpose and support each other at every stage.

## FAQ

### English

Q: Do I need prior dragon boat experience?
A: No. We welcome beginners and support paddlers at all levels.

Q: Is this team only for women?
A: Yes. We are an all-women team focused on creating a supportive environment.

Q: What should I bring to practice?
A: Athletic wear and water. Additional details will be shared before sessions.

Q: Is the team competitive?
A: Yes. We train seriously, while maintaining a supportive and respectful team culture.

Q: How can I join?
A: Contact us via email or Instagram for current programs and trial sessions.
